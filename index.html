NFL TEAMS

COWBOYS The Dallas Cowboys hold the NFL record for most consecutive winning seasons with 20 from 1966 to 1985. During that time, they reached the playoffs 18 times, won 13 NFC East championships, and reached the Super Bowl five times.

GREEN BAY PACKERS

The Green Bay Packers are a professional American football team based in Green Bay, Wisconsin. The Packers compete in the National Football League as a member club of the National Football Conference North division.

49ERS

The San Francisco 49ers are a professional American football team based in the San Francisco Bay Area.
